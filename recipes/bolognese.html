Bolognese Recipe

Back to home

Delicious looking home-made bolognese


Ingredients

  • 1 Pound lean group beef
  • 3 ounces bacon, chopped
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup sliced celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • Spaghetti Pasta

Steps

  1. Brown beef, bacon, onion, celery and carrots in a large saucepan over medium-high heat until browned and crisp, about 10 minutes.

  2. Add broth; simmer until evaporated, scraping browned bits from bottom of pan.

  3. Stir in tomatoes and milk. Simmer over medium-low heat until sauce is thickened, about 15 minutes. Serve over pasta.
